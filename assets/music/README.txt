Put 2-5 royalty-free MP3 background music tracks in this folder. The bot picks one at random for each video and plays it quietly under the voiceover.

Where to get genuinely royalty-free tracks (free, no copyright strikes):
- YouTube Audio Library (studio.youtube.com -> Audio Library) — built for this exact use case, download directly as mp3
- Pixabay Music (pixabay.com/music) — free for commercial use, no attribution required
- Free Music Archive (freemusicarchive.org) — filter by CC0 / no-attribution license

Avoid anything pulled from a random video or "no copyright music" YouTube reupload —
those are not reliably licensed even when labeled that way. Get tracks directly from
the sources above so you have a clean license trail if YouTube ever asks.

If this folder is empty, the bot just skips music and uses voiceover only — nothing breaks.
